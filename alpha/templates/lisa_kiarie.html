Lisa Kiarie Foundation

Lisa Kiarie Epilepsy Awareness and Support Foundation

What we Do?

We create awareness for epilepsy to eliminate the stagnation and ideologies surrounding it.

Mission

Create awareness for epilepsy to eliminate the stagnation and ideologies surrounding it. Make known the in-depth details of the condition. Spread public awareness that this is a condition and not a way of life. Epilepsy is maybe the abnormality in the normality that can certainly be changed.

Vision

A Universal pillar for epileptic individuals. A center that provides support from the very evident physical afflictions to the least obvious social and mental effects of the condition.
